About GYAAN 2K26

GYAAN 2K26 is a National Level Technical Symposium organized by the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering at JJ College of Engineering and Technology, Trichy. The symposium brings together talented students from different institutions to showcase innovation, creativity, and technical skills through various technical and non-technical competitions.
