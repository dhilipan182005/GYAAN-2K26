About Symposium

GYAAN 2K26 is a national level technical symposium organized by the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering at JJ College of Engineering and Technology, Trichy. The event brings together innovative students from various colleges to showcase their technical skills through competitions, quizzes, and exciting activities.

About JJ College of Engineering and Technology

JJ College of Engineering and Technology, Trichy, is one of the leading engineering institutions in Tamil Nadu. The college is committed to excellence in education, innovation, and research while preparing students to meet global technological challenges.
